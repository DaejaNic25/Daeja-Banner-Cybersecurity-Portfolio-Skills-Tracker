# Home Network Asset Inventory

This project was completed as part of the Google Cybersecurity Certificate on Coursera.

## Objective
To identify and classify assets connected to a home network and assess their sensitivity.

## Assets Identified

| Asset        | Network Access | Owner | Location      | Notes | Sensitivity |
|-------------|--------------|------|--------------|------|------------|
| Laptop       | Always       | Me   | Home Office  | Stores business data and credentials | Confidential |
| Smartphone   | Always       | Me   | Mobile       | Contains personal data and MFA apps | Confidential |
| Smart TV     | Always       | Me   | Living Room  | Connected to Wi-Fi; potential IoT risk | Internal |

## Key Takeaways
- Devices storing sensitive data require higher classification
- Always-connected devices increase attack surface
- IoT devices often have weaker security controls

## Skills Demonstrated
- Asset Inventory Management
- Risk Classification
- Basic Security Assessment
